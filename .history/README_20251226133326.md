### 2.1.2 One-Line Summary

A threat hunting case study tracking a **CEO impersonation phishing attack targeting employee VPN credentials** at the cryptocurrency exchange **Red Raccoon Coin**.

---

### 2.1.3 Detailed Summary

On November 1st, 2025, a phishing email impersonating the CEO was distributed to employees of the cryptocurrency exchange **Red Raccoon Coin**.
The email contained a link to a **cloned internal welfare portal**, prompting victims to log in using their **corporate VPN credentials**.

The CEO denied sending the message and requested a formal investigation by the Threat Intelligence (TI) team on November 2nd, 2025.
To assess the compromise scope and prevent secondary damage, the TI team conducted an **adversary tracking investigation** on November 15th, 2025.

The reconstructed attack scenario is summarized as follows:

- Victims were redirected to a **Lure Site** via a phishing email link
- Requests from the lure site were forwarded through a public **Redirector server** to a hidden **C2 (Team Server)** using an **HTTP redirect chain**
- The attacker leveraged **Tor-based ONION services** and **GitHub accounts** to build a multi-layered infrastructure and evade attribution
